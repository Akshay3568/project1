About Naipunnya Institute of Management and Information Technology

The Naipunnya Institute of Management and Information Technology is located in an eco-friendly campus ensuring that every student steps in the right path and fulfills all their dreams under the guidance of eminent faculty members. With a basic motto of filling society with educated and well-trained individuals, the management teaches students with a high-standard academic structure. The management grooms every student by following numerous innovative teaching techniques and methodologies.

Facts and Figures

  • Year of Establishment: 1998
  • Approval: AICTE
  • Institute Affiliated to: University of Calicut
  • Total Number of Companies Visited: 28+
  • How to Reach:
    • Airport: Reaching the college campus in a prepaid taxi from the Cochin International Airport is the easiest way and doesn’t consume much time.
    • Railway Station: Irinjalakkuda Railway Station is 17.1 km away from the campus, and one can hire a vehicle to reach the college.
    • Bus Stand: Thrissur bus stand is 41.2 km from the college, and one can reach by local transportation.

Why Join Naipunnya Institute of Management and Information Technology?

  • Facilities: The campus offers modern facilities such as finely ventilated classrooms, a well-stocked library, and laboratories to help students concentrate on their academics and improve their skills.
  • Placement Cell: The college has a dedicated placement cell that supports every student to secure a better placement. The cell organizes events and activities to ensure students' overall development.
  • Industrial Training: The college provides professional exposure by organizing industry visits, allowing students to learn techniques useful during placements.
