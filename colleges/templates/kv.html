About Sree Kerala Varma College

Established: 1947

Sree Kerala Varma College was established with a mission of strong commitment to bring up students from rural and semi-urban areas, with poor socio-economic backgrounds, on the same level as those from better facilitated urban areas. The college strives to make students competent to meet the challenges of the modern world with full social commitment and confidence. The college is one of the most well-known educational institutions in Kerala and is the biggest Science and Arts College under the University of Calicut. The college aims to preserve the best traditions of progressive higher education, ensuring complete freedom of expression to encounter diverse ideological preoccupations.

Facts and Figures

  • Year of Establishment: 1947

Facilities

The library is fully computerized with modern software. Internet connectivity is available, and a collection of Braille books is provided. The campus offers a corporate feel with top-of-the-line furniture. The women's hostel is located on-campus with good facilities and minimal fees.
