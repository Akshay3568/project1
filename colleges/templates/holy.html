About Holy Grace Academy of Engineering

Holy Grace Academy of Engineering for Women operates under the benefaction of Holy Grace Foundation. The most important mission of the academy is the exposure of future engineers and scientists to scientific methods of problem evaluation, simulation, modeling, and identification. Empowering women in various professional courses is what the academy aims to achieve. Apart from this, developing students with integrated and disciplined personalities and achieving academic excellence also form the main goals of the academy.

Facts and Figures

  • Year of Establishment: 2010
  • Approval: All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)
  • Campus Strength: 240
  • Total Faculty Members: 50
  • How to Reach: 2.5 km away from Mala Town

Why Choose Holy Grace Academy of Engineering for Women?

  • Foreign Study Trips Completely Free of Cost: Students get the option of visiting international trade fairs at no cost.
  • Management Seats for Disqualified Students: Students who get disqualified in entrance exams can avail management seats.
  • Exclusive Add-On Courses: The institute offers add-on courses, including SAP, across various branches.
  • Reputable Faculty: Faculties from IITs, foreign universities, and even former HODs.
  • Soft Skill and Communicative English Training: Training is provided to develop soft skills and communication skills.
  • Top Placements: Regular PDPs, training sessions, expert lectures, and industrial visits are conducted by the Placement and Career Guidance Cell.
