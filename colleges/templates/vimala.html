About Vimala College

Written By: Collegedunia Team

Vimala College is a Roman Catholic women’s college located in Thrissur city of Kerala, India. It was established in 1967 and bifurcated from St. Mary’s College, now affiliated with the University of Calicut. The college is accredited by NAAC and approved by UGC.

Vimala College offers 18 UG and 13 PG programs and is a Center for Research in English, Physics, Commerce, Social Work, and Economics. Admissions are merit-based for most of the courses.

Vimala College Admissions

Admission to Vimala College is merit-based for most courses, with entrance-based admission for MSW and Ph.D. programs. The process is available both online and offline. Candidates can fill out the online application form or get it directly from the institution.

Application Process

  1. Visit the official website of Vimala College
  2. Download and fill out the application form
  3. Make payment through online or offline mode
  4. Merit list will be published based on qualifying exam or entrance exam scores
  5. If needed, attend an interview or group discussion
