About St. Mary's College

Written By: Collegedunia Team

St. Mary's College is the First Women's College of Thrissur District set up by The Congregation of Mother of Carmel (CMC). The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) ranked St. Mary’s College at 55 for the year 2017. The women’s college offers various Undergraduate, Postgraduate, Vocational and Certificate programs. The courses offered include BA, B.Sc., BCA, B.Com, BBA, BSW and BVOC at the Undergraduate level and MA, M.Sc., and M.Com. programs at the postgraduate level. The selection procedure is entirely merit-based. The application mode for the admission is both online as well as offline.

St. Mary's College was established in 1946 and is affiliated with Calicut University. It offers a wide range of programs across arts, science, and commerce disciplines. The college provides a strong academic foundation and offers numerous facilities to help students excel in their studies and extra-curricular activities.

St. Mary's College Admissions

Admission to St. Mary’s College is merit-based. The University of Calicut follows a Centralized Admission Process (CAP) for undergraduate admissions. Interested candidates must fill out the online application form and follow the necessary steps as outlined by the college.

How to Apply?

  1. Visit the official website of the College
  2. Download and fill out the application form
  3. Make the payment via online or offline through challan generation
  4. Merit list will be published after submission
  5. Attend the interview with the necessary documents (Mark Sheets, Transfer Certificate, etc.)

St. Mary's College Scholarships

The college offers various scholarships and financial assistance to students based on merit and need. Some of the scholarships available include:

  • Post Matric Scholarship For Minorities (PMS)
  • Central Sector Scholarship (CSS)
  • State Merit Scholarship (SMS)
  • District Merit Scholarship (DMS)
  • Suvarna Jubilee Merit Scholarship (SJMS)
  • Music Fine Arts Scholarship (MFAS)
  • Post-Graduate Indira Gandhi Scholarship for Single Girl Child
