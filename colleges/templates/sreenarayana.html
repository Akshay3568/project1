About Sree Narayana College Nattika

Written By: Sapna Malik

Sree Narayana College Nattika was established in 1967 and is affiliated with the University of Calicut. The College was accredited by NAAC and offers UG, PG, and PhD programmes in numerous specializations. It also offers Diploma and Certification programmes. The College was placed in the 150-200 rank band in the NIRF rankings of 2020, and it was ranked #151 among the top colleges in India.

Admissions in UG and PG Programmes in all affiliated colleges (including Sree Narayana College) are done through the Single Window System (SWS) implemented by the University of Calicut. Students wishing to apply must fill out the online application form through the official university website.

Sree Narayana College Admissions

Admissions to all courses at Sree Narayana College are conducted through the Common Admission Programme of the University of Calicut. Candidates can apply online through the official University website:

  • www.universityofcalicut.info
  • www.cuonline.ac.in

After filling out the form online and uploading the required documents, candidates must submit the form and take a printout for future reference.

Sree Narayana College Scholarships

The College offers various scholarships such as:

  • Suvarna Jubilee Merit Scholarship (SJMS): For UG and PG students from BPL families with a minimum of 50% marks in the qualifying exam.
  • Post Metric Scholarship (PMS): For Muslim and Latin converted Christian communities.
  • State Merit Scholarship: For students with 50% or above in the qualifying examination and annual family income below 1 Lac.

Sree Narayana College Facilities

  • Library: A 6000 sq. ft. building with over 46,000 books and journals.
  • Computer Centre: 120+ computers with high-speed LAN and eight mini-computer labs.
  • Hostel: Accommodates 75 girls students with a peaceful environment.
  • Fitness Centre: Equipped with basic fitness equipment and playfields.
  • Canteen: Provides vegetarian and non-vegetarian food at reasonable prices.
  • Capability Enhancement Centre: Language labs, remedial coaching, career counselling, etc.

Sree Narayana College Faculty

The College has over 60 dedicated and highly knowledgeable teaching staff members, including Associate and Assistant Professors with over three years of teaching experience.

Reviews

Students appreciate the library, campus, and the quality of teaching. However, some students have expressed concerns about exams, departmental restrictions, and certain aspects of the college environment.
