About Vidya Academy of Science and Technology (VAST)

Vidya Academy of Science and Technology (VAST) is located in Kerala, India, and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The college is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and is part of the Vidya International Charitable Trust.

VAST offers B.Tech as an undergraduate program and M.Tech and MCA as post-graduate courses. The institution provides a fee waiver option and admits students based on entrance exams. The application fee is INR 500/-.

Facts and Figures:

  • Established: 2003
  • Affiliated to: APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University
  • Accreditation: AICTE approved
  • Courses Offered: B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA
  • Facilities: Library, hostel, labs, auditorium
  • Location: Thrissur, Kerala

Admissions

Admissions to B.Tech are based on the Kerala Engineering Architecture Medical (KEAM) exam. For lateral B.Tech admissions, candidates must appear in the Lateral Entry Test (LET). M.Tech admissions are based on GATE exam scores, and MCA admissions are determined through the MCA Entrance Exam of Kerala.

The application process includes filling out the online form, paying the application fee of INR 500/-, and submitting the necessary documents. Selected candidates will be called for document verification.
